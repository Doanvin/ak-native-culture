Culture Camp 2018

This Culture Camp will take place March 24, 2018 in Isleton, CA.

Event Details

Please be ready to choose your projects due to the limited time we have. All participants will go home with a drum and drumstick plus two projects.

  1. Deer skin Drum with Donavin Hannon and Ryan Kozisek
  2. Drum Stick
  3. Small Paddle
  4. Native Art with Tlingit Artist Doug Chilton from Juneau, Alaska
  5. Beading project with Natalie Suan and Barbara Nix
  6. Headdress, Octopus Bag with Robin Montano
  7. Devils Club Beaded necklace
  8. Haida Language by Rob Yates from Creswell, Oregon (Craig, Alaska)
  9. Weaving Project with Marilyn Nix

Dinner to follow at 5:30pm with Haida name giving and Southern Winds Dance Group performing. Free -- any donations will go to Southern Winds Dance Group.

What: 2018 Culture Camp
When: March 24th, 2018 | 10AM - 5PM
Where: Isleton Community Hall
210 Jackson Blvd Exd, Isleton California
Cost: $40 per person + $10 refundable cleaning fee at the door
